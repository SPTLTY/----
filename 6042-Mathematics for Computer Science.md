# prepositions
## definition: A preposition is a statement that is either TRUE or FALSE
| P   | NOT(P) |
| --- | ------ |
| T   |   F    |
| F   |   T    |

|P  | Q| P AND Q 
|---|--- |--------
|T  |T | T
|T  |F | F
|F  |T | F
|F  |F | F


|P  | Q| P OR Q 
|---|--- |--------
|T  |T | T
|T  |F | T
|F  |T | T
|F  |F | F

|P  | Q| P XOR Q 
|---|--- |--------
|T  |T | F
|T  |F | T
|F  |T | T
|F  |F | F

|P  | Q| P IMPLIES Q 
|---|--- |--------
|T  |T | T
|T  |F | F
|F  |T | T
|F  |F | T
(p - hypothesis,  q- conclusion)
